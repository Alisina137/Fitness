You are a senior product engineer building a premium AI fitness application.

The foundation and architecture are already created. Now build the complete user onboarding and fitness profile system.

The goal:
Create an onboarding experience that collects enough information to allow future AI systems to generate highly personalized workouts, nutrition plans, and coaching.

## 1. Create a Premium Onboarding Flow

Build a multi-step onboarding wizard.

Requirements:

* Beautiful mobile-first UI
* Progress indicator
* Back/Next navigation
* Save progress automatically
* Smooth transitions
* Validation on every step
* Allow users to edit answers later from profile settings

The onboarding should feel similar to premium apps like:

* Apple Fitness+
* Freeletics
* Whoop
* Oura

## 2. Onboarding Questions

Create the following steps:

### Step 1: Welcome

Display:

* Personalized welcome message
* Explain that the app will create a fitness journey based on the user's lifestyle

Example:
"Let's build your personalized fitness plan."

---

### Step 2: Basic Information

Collect:

* Full name
* Age
* Gender
* Height
* Weight

Store this data in the User model.

---

### Step 3: Main Fitness Goal

Allow selecting one primary goal:

Options:

* Lose fat
* Build muscle
* Increase strength
* Improve endurance
* Improve general health
* Maintain current fitness

Allow secondary goals.

---

### Step 4: Fitness Experience

Options:

* Beginner
* Intermediate
* Advanced
* Athlete

Store fitness level.

---

### Step 5: Current Activity Level

Options:

* Sedentary
* Lightly active
* Moderately active
* Very active
* Extremely active

---

### Step 6: Workout Preferences

Collect:

Workout location:

* Home
* Gym
* Outdoor
* Mixed

Available equipment:

Examples:

* No equipment
* Dumbbells
* Resistance bands
* Barbell
* Machines
* Full gym

Allow multiple selections.

---

### Step 7: Workout Availability

Collect:

Days per week:

* 1 day

* 4 days
* 5 days
* 6 days
* Every day

Average workout duration:

* 10 minutes
* 20 minutes

* 60+ minutes

Collect:

Injuries:

* None

* Back

* No preference

* Vegetarian
* Vegan

* Mediterranean
* Low carb

* Allergies
* Foods avoided

### Step 10: Motivation & Lifestyle

Why do you want to get fit?

Options:

* Look better
* Feel healthier
* Build confidence
* Improve performance
* Prepare for an event

Sleep average:

* Less than 5 hours
* 5-7 hours
* 7-9 hours
* 9+ hours
