## Fat Loss

Prioritize:

* Full-body workouts
* Compound exercises
* Supersets where appropriate
* Moderate rest
* High calorie expenditure

---

## Muscle Gain

Prioritize:

* Progressive overload
* Hypertrophy volume
* Muscle group frequency
* Compound + isolation balance
* Recovery planning

---

## Strength

Prioritize:

* Heavy compound lifts
* Lower rep ranges
* Longer rest
* Strength progression

---

## Endurance

Prioritize:

* Cardio
* Conditioning
* Work capacity
* Longer training duration

---

## General Health

Prioritize:

* Balanced movement
* Mobility
* Cardiovascular fitness
* Functional strength

# 7. Progressive Overload Intelligence

Analyze workout history.

If user consistently succeeds:

Increase:

* Weight
* Repetitions
* Sets
* Exercise difficulty

If user struggles:

Decrease:

* Volume
* Intensity
* Complexity

If user misses workouts:

Automatically adapt the following week's training.

Example:

User missed 8 days.

Recommendation:

Reduce intensity by 20%.
